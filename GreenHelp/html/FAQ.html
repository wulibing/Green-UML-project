Q: Where is the palette? I open up a new diagram and I don't see the palette mentioned in the help documentation and shown in pictures, but I can't find when I load a diagram.
A: The palette can be hidden. If it is hidden, it is shrunk to a small bar on one side of the diagram editor. You can click on the the small bar on the side of the current diagram editor to unhide the palette. You can also drag the palette to the other side if you wish.
